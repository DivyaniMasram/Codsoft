VARIETIES

Amber apple

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

Kesar

The Kesar mango has a distinctive fragrance to it that can liven up your house. It is grown and cultivated in the Girnar mountains of Gujarat, Ahmedabad and is named after the spice ‘saffron’. Season: June to July

Delicious Oranges

Nagpur orange is a variety of orange grown in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. It is rustic and pockmarked exterior which is sweet and has juicy pulp. It gives the city of Nagpur its pseudonym Orange City.

Grapes

Blessed with nature's bounty, Nashik is the grapes heartland of India. With nearly a century old tradition of grape growing, Nashik farmers produce a large variety of grapes. Using a blend of modern technology with tradition, the Nashik grower has perfected the art of growing high quality grapes.
